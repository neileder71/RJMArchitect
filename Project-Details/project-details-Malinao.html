Malinao Resort

A completed 4-month resort development offering premium accommodations and leisure facilities in Barangay Luna.

Duration 4 months
Rooms 24 units
Location Barangay Luna, Siargao Island

Project Overview

Malinao Resort is a premier 4-month resort development in Barangay Luna, featuring 24 comfortable guest units designed to provide an exceptional island experience. The resort combines modern amenities with tropical aesthetics, creating an inviting retreat for tourists and visitors.

The facility includes well-appointed accommodations, dining areas, recreational spaces, and guest services designed to deliver world-class resort hospitality. Every aspect of the property was planned to maximize guest satisfaction while maintaining operational efficiency and sustainable practices.

Project Highlights

Building a resort in a tropical island location required careful management of environmental factors, construction schedules, and resort facility standards. Our team overcame these challenges through experienced planning and dedicated execution.

Climate-Resilient Construction

Designed and built structures resistant to tropical weather conditions while maintaining comfort and durability standards required for guest accommodations and facilities.

Expedited Project Timeline

Completed the entire resort within 4 months through efficient resource coordination, skilled workforce management, and strategic construction sequencing.

Guest Experience Design

Integrated leisure facilities, accommodations, and common areas to create cohesive resort spaces that enhance the guest experience and operational workflow.

Designing meaningful spaces through innovative concepts and timeless architectural solutions.
