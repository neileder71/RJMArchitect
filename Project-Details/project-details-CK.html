CK Residence

An in-progress 12-month residential building project in Barangay Luna combining modern design with quality construction standards.

Duration 12 months
Area 360 sq.m
Location Barangay Luna, Surigao City

Project Overview

CK Residence is an in-progress 12-month residential building project in Barangay Luna, Surigao City. The 360 sq.m development is being constructed with careful attention to modern design principles and quality standards, delivering a contemporary residential property.

The project incorporates modern living spaces, efficient floor plans, and quality finishes to provide residents with comfortable, contemporary accommodation. The 12-month construction timeline ensures methodical execution and careful quality management throughout all phases of development.

Project Highlights

Developing a modern residential building in Surigao City required balancing contemporary design aspirations with practical construction considerations. Our team successfully navigated project complexities to deliver an exceptional result.

Modern Design Integration

Created contemporary living spaces with efficient layouts, adequate natural lighting, and functional design that enhances resident quality of life.

Construction Quality Standards

Maintained consistent quality standards throughout the 12-month construction period with rigorous inspection and materials testing protocols.

Timely Project Execution

Efficiently coordinated all construction phases to maintain schedule while ensuring no compromises on quality and building performance standards.
