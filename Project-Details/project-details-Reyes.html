Reyes Renovation

Professional renovation and restoration project in Surigao City combining modern updates with preservation of original architectural elements.

Duration 6 months
Area 380 sq.m
Location Surigao City

Project Overview

The Reyes Renovation project involved comprehensive restoration and modernization of an existing structure in Surigao City. The 6-month renovation carefully balanced the preservation of original architectural character with modern safety and functionality upgrades.

The project included structural updates, modern systems installation, aesthetic improvements, and preservation of distinctive original architectural features. This renovation project demonstrates how thoughtful restoration can bring new life to existing structures while maintaining their historic and aesthetic value.

Project Highlights

Renovation projects present unique challenges including structural assessment of existing conditions, preservation of original elements, and integration of modern systems. Our team addressed these complexities with expertise and careful planning.

Structural Assessment & Retrofit

Conducted thorough structural evaluation and implemented necessary retrofitting to meet modern building codes while preserving original architectural character and integrity.

Modern Systems Integration

Seamlessly incorporated contemporary electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and safety systems into the existing structure without compromising original design elements.

Aesthetic Restoration

Restored and enhanced original architectural features while updating finishes and materials to provide improved aesthetics and functional performance for modern use.

Designing meaningful spaces through innovative concepts and timeless architectural solutions.
