BDO UNIBANK

The first BDO Unibank branch in Siargao Island, designed to serve the local community with modern banking facilities.

Duration 4 months
Area 300 sq.m
Location Dapa, Siargao Island

Project Overview

The BDO Unibank Siargao Branch represents a significant milestone as the first banking facility of this kind to be established on Siargao Island. This modern commercial building was designed to provide comprehensive banking services to the local community and visiting tourists, combining functionality with contemporary architectural aesthetics.

Located in Brgy. Osmeña, Municipality of Dapa, the facility was completed within a 4-month timeframe, demonstrating efficient project management and coordination. The branch features modern banking amenities, secure vault facilities, and customer service areas designed for optimal workflow and security compliance.

Project Highlights

Constructing a modern banking facility on Siargao Island presented unique challenges including isolated location, limited local resources, and the need for specialized security infrastructure. Our team successfully navigated these obstacles through strategic planning and innovative solutions.

Secure Infrastructure Design

Implemented advanced vault systems, reinforced security features, and climate-controlled storage to meet banking institution standards and regulatory requirements.

Efficient Timeline Management

Coordinated construction to be completed within 4 months despite island logistics, sourcing materials efficiently and maintaining work schedules at optimal productivity.

Community Integration

Designed the facility to complement the local environment while providing modern amenities and accessible banking services to the Siargao Island community.

Designing meaningful spaces through innovative concepts and timeless architectural solutions.
